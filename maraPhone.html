What is Mara phone?

Mara phone is a platform for the future of the people, youth and adults as well.

While existing solutions offer to solve just one problem at a time, our team is up to build a secure, useful, & easy-to-use product selling platform, that is fully integrated with a cryptocurrency based on the etherscan blockchain. It will include easy cryptocurrency payments integration, fast exchanges from one crypto to another or crypto to fiat & also vice-versa, and even a digital arbitration system.
