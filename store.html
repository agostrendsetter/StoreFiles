What is 17track?

17track is an online platform designed to track any product shipped from the seller to the buyer.

What kind of shipment does it track?

Depending on the sellers shipping methods, whether it is sent through via airmail or sea level, 17track is capable of tracking any kind of shipping method, provided the seller gives tracking details for the buyer to keep track of his/her product(s) purchased.

How does 17track track products when purchased?

When the seller ships the product, he/she gives the correct tracking details of the product to the buyer, however 17track uses the tracking details to locate how far the product is from the buyer to seller after it is shipped.
